Description

The random experiment consists of tossing \(n\) coins, each with probability of heads \(p\). Random variable \(Y\) gives the number of heads and has the binomial distribution with parameters \(n\) and \(p\). The probability density function and moments of \(Y\) are shown in blue in the distribution graph blue and are recorded in the distribution table. On each run, the empirical density function and moments of \(Y\) are shown in red in the distribution graph and are recorded in the distribution table. The parameters \(n\) and \(p\) can be varied with scroll bars.
